Ming Zhang

University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, School of Electronic, Electrical and Communication Engineering.

  • He received his B.E. degree in Measurement & Control Technology and Instrumentation from China University of Geoscience, Beijing, China, in 2019.
  • He is currently working toward his Ph.D. degree in Signal and Information Processing from School of Electronic, Electrical and Communication Engineering, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China.
  • He is also working toward his Ph.D. degree in Signal and Information Processing from Institute of Acoustics, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China.
  • He is co-advised by Professor Jun Zhou, Professor Qingwei Zhao and Professor Yonghong Yan in the CAS Key Laboratory of Speech Acoustics and Content Understanding.
  • His research interests include natural language process, fintech, and circuit implementation.
